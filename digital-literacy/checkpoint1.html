Checkpoint - How Will Technology Help Me?

Use the previous readings to help answer the following questions:

  1. How will technology help me in my personal life?
    (Two or three sentences)

  2. How will technology help me in my professional life?
    (Two or three sentences)

  3. Why is it important to learn about digital technology? What are the benefits of using computers and the Internet? What are the dangers or potential downsides? Give several examples.
    (Four to five sentences)

  4. What are you hoping to learn from this course? Do you have any questions about computers or technology?
    (Two or three sentences)

Write your answers to the above questions in a word document and send it to the tech coach.
